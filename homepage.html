How to Donate Blood?

  1. Find a blood donation center or a blood drive.
  2. Make sure you meet the health and age criteria.
  3. Bring a valid ID to your appointment.
  4. Follow the pre-donation instructions.
  5. Donate blood - the process usually takes about 30 minutes.
  6. Enjoy refreshments and rest for a short period after donating.
